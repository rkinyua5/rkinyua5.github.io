Akan Name Generator

This is a web application that takes your birthday details and calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender, outputs your Akan Name derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'Day Name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

To find out what your Akan name is, Fill the form below and click generate name.
